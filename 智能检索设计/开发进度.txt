## 当前主链形态

- 结构化 / 知识混合链：
  - `parse_intent(Python LangChain 优先) -> action_review(Python review_action 优先) -> normalize_params(Java) -> route_tools(Python LangGraph 优先) -> execute(Java authority) -> compose_response(Python composer 优先，Java RuntimeAnswerComposer 兜底)`
- 实时链：
  - `parse_intent(Python LangChain 优先) -> action_review(Python review_action 优先) -> realtime_query(Java orchestrator) -> realtime answer`
